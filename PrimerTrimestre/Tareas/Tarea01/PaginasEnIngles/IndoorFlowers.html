Indoor Flowers!

In this section, you can find a wide variety of indoor flowers.

Monstera deliciosa

Tropical plant with large, perforated leaves. Adds an exotic and elegant touch. Needs bright indirect light and moderate watering; allow the soil to dry between waterings.

Chinese Money Plant

Plant with round, shiny leaves, a symbol of prosperity and good luck. Needs indirect light and moderate watering; allow the soil to dry between waterings.

Violin Fig

Plant with violin-shaped leaves, ideal for bright interiors. Needs indirect light and moderate watering; avoid moving it frequently.

Snake Plant

Plant with long, pointed leaves, hardy and easy to care for. Needs indirect light and moderate watering.

Calathea

Plant with large, showy leaves, featuring colorful patterns and shapes. Needs indirect light and moderate watering; mist its leaves to maintain humidity.

Philodendron

Climbing or hanging plant with heart-shaped leaves in various shades of green. Prefers indirect light and moderate watering, allowing the soil to dry slightly between waterings.

Boston Fern

Lush plant with long, fine leaves that give a lush and fresh appearance. Needs indirect light and high humidity, making it ideal for bathrooms; water regularly.

Croton

Colorful plant with leaves in shades of green, yellow, red, and orange. Requires bright light and moderate watering, avoiding sudden temperature changes.

Peace Lily

Elegant plant with shiny green leaves and white flowers that purify the air. Tolerates low light but blooms better with indirect light; water when the soil is dry.

English Ivy

Climbing plant ideal for hanging in pots or shelves, with star-shaped leaves. Prefers indirect light and moderate watering; avoid letting the soil dry out too much.
