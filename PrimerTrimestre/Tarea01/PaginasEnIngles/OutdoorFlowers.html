Outdoor Flowers!

In this section, you will find a wide variety of outdoor flowers.

Roses

Roses are popular ornamental shrubs known for their beautiful and fragrant flowers, which come in a variety of colors and sizes. They are ideal for gardens and floral arrangements due to their durability and appeal. For their care, they require at least 6 hours of daily sunlight, well-drained and nutrient-rich soil. They should be watered regularly, keeping the soil moist but not waterlogged, and they need annual pruning in winter to encourage their growth and flowering.

Geraniums

Plants with vibrant and long-lasting colors, ideal for pots and gardens. They require direct sunlight, moderate watering, and pruning to stimulate continuous blooming.

Lavender

Aromatic shrub with purple flowers, perfect for sunny areas and well-drained soils. It needs little water and is pruned after flowering to maintain its shape.

Jasmine

Climbing plant with fragrant white flowers, it needs indirect light and moist, nutrient-rich soils. It requires light pruning and frequent watering without waterlogging.

Petunia

With colorful and abundant flowers, they thrive in sunlight and regular watering. They are ideal for pots and benefit from light pruning to stimulate growth.

Hydrangeas

Shrubs with large clusters of flowers, they need partial shade, acidic, and moist soil. They are pruned in winter and require abundant watering in warm periods.

Carnations

Plants with fragrant and striking flowers, which prefer partial sun and well-drained soils. They are watered moderately and dead flowers are pruned.

Daisies

With simple and cheerful flowers, they grow well in sunlight and light soils. They require moderate watering and dead flower pruning to prolong blooming.
