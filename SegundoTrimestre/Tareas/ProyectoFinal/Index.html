Hyperborea

In Greek mythology, Hyperborea was a region situated in the as yet unknown northern lands, north of Thrace. Its name (from the Greek (Υπερ βορεία) Hyper Boreia ‘beyond the north’) derives precisely from the fact that the wind-god Boreas was believed to dwell in Hyperborea.

Hyperborea

Beyond the north, in northern lands unknown to mankind. They never found our home for it was everywhere but nowhere at the same time. We are where Boreas, god of the north wind, is. Where is he? They place us above the Arctic Circle, where the Aurora Borealis are born and they are still wrong.
